Sponsors

Sponsorship Tiers and Benefits

In recognition of the support, sponsors will be acknowledged and featured on:

  • the website of ACISP 2025
  • the conference venue and pack
  • Something else we can easily do for you.

To acknowledge more generous support, we will assign the following sponsorship level designations based on the amount of contribution.

Platinum level - AUD $13,000 and up (receive three free registrations)

Gold level - AUD $10,000 and up (receive two free registrations)

Silver level - AUD $6,000 and up (receive one free registration)

Bronze level - AUD $3,000 and up

Note: order and size of the logos displayed may depend on the sponsorship level. Please contact the General Chairs if your company is interested in sponsoring this conference.
